<h1>HOA CONNECT</h1>

<p>
<strong>HOA CONNECT</strong> is a community-focused application designed exclusively for
Homeowners Association (HOA) managed subdivisions and residential communities.
</p>

<hr />

<h2>Purpose</h2>

<p>
HOA CONNECT aims to improve communication, transparency, and efficiency within a
subdivision by providing digital tools for both residents and HOA administrators.
</p>

<p>
The application is intended for <strong>internal use only</strong> and is accessible
exclusively to registered residents and authorized administrators of the subdivision.
</p>

<hr />

<h2>Key Features</h2>

<h3>For Residents</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Personal resident profile with address and ownership details</li>
  <li>Marketplace where residents can list items or services for sale</li>
  <li>Request board for posting service requests (e.g., repairs, assistance, services)</li>
  <li>View subdivision announcements and important notices</li>
  <li>Track personal HOA-related bills and payment records</li>
  <li>View availability status of units (for rent or sale)</li>
  <li>Secure access limited to registered subdivision residents</li>
</ul>

<h3>For HOA Administrators</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Master list of registered residents</li>
  <li>Resident profile and ownership management</li>
  <li>Billing management per resident, including:</li>
  <ul>
    <li>Monthly association dues</li>
    <li>Security / guard salaries</li>
    <li>Maintenance fees</li>
    <li>Other subdivision-related charges</li>
  </ul>
  <li>Posting official announcements for residents</li>
  <li>Monitoring of community activity and engagement</li>
  <li>Centralized data management for the subdivision</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<h2>Marketplace & Requests</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Residents can post items or services they wish to sell within the community</li>
  <li>Residents can post service requests for other residents or service providers</li>
  <li>Community-only visibility to maintain privacy and relevance</li>
  <li>Encourages local transactions and collaboration within the subdivision</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<h2>Security & Access</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Access is restricted to authenticated users only</li>
  <li>Resident accounts are validated against the HOA master resident list</li>
  <li>Administrative features are limited to authorized HOA personnel</li>
  <li>Resident data is protected and accessible only within the subdivision</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<h2>Target Users</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Homeowners</li>
  <li>Tenants</li>
  <li>HOA Officers</li>
  <li>Subdivision Administrators</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<h2>Technology Overview</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Mobile application built using Flutter</li>
  <li>Backend services powered by Firebase</li>
  <li>Real-time data updates for profiles, announcements, and activities</li>
  <li>Secure authentication and role-based access</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<h2>Disclaimer</h2>

<p>
HOA CONNECT is intended solely for use within a homeowners association or managed
residential subdivision. It is not designed for public communities or open-access platforms.
</p>
